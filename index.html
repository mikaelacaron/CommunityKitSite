What's our goal?

Last year the developer community made the most of their WWDC week at CommunityKit, located at the intersection of innovation and celebration. We gathered at the Hyatt House Cupertino to share with your fellow participants, learn so many new things, and build some great memories.

Each day we were joined by our wonderful communities, such as Hacking With Swift, iOSDevHappyHour, try! Swift, and WWDCScholars to name a few.

We also hosted LIVE podcast recordings!

  • EmpowerApps.Show with Leo Dion
  • Swift over Coffee with Paul Hudson and Mikaela Caron
  • Launched with Charlie Chapman

    • The collaboration within the indie dev community has always been a prime example of how we build one another up, especially since we often have to be both developers and designers. Last year we hosted our very first Indie Fair where indies of all backgrounds came to show off their current project. It could have been an app, design, or anything in between. The results were smashing and we had an amazing time together.

    As you can see, we had a lot of fun and great experiences last year. We can't wait until we can all get together again and put the Community in CommunityKit 2026.

    Getting ready for WWDC

    WWDC26 doesn't have official dates yet, but here's what you can expect

    Keynote & SOTU Watch Party

    No Golden Ticket? No problem. Join the annual tradition of watching the Keynote and SOTU alongside the iOSDevHappyHour and WWDC Community in Cupertino. This year, we're turning up the energy at our official home base: CommunityKit HQ.

    Connect & Plan with the Community

    Connect with fellow attendees in Cupertino through our official Discord group! Plan activities, share tips on things like shared housing and food, and start making new friends—even before the event begins.

    Stay Updated

    Be the first to know—register your interest or follow us on Mastodon at @CommunityKit.mastodon.social, Bluesky at @CommunityKit.bsky.social, or X at @communitykit_ for the latest announcements and updates.

    Bring the Community Together

    Whether you're into code, design, or big ideas, passion thrives when it's shared. Rally the community, start the conversations, and create something that pushes boundaries. We have a space for the Apple Developer Community.
